Best Buy Price Compare

Useful tool to quickly pricematch while shopping at Best Buy. Simply enter the URL of a product you're looking at and Price Comparer will find the eligible price matches for you all on one page!

Timezone Converter

While in serious need of a facelift, Timezone Converter can automatically convert time between to places. Have a meeting with folks in Beijing at 10 AM? Timezone Converter will let you know the local time while taking into effect DST in both locations.
